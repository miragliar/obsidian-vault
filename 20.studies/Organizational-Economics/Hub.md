## 🗂️ Inhalts-Übersicht (Course Outline)

```
1. Plattformen: Definitionen und Kernkonzepte
   ├── Plattform-Definition (Aguiar-Standard)
   ├── Network Effects: WGNE/CGNE, ±positiv
   ├── Wertschöpfung & Wertextraktion
   └── Typologie

2. Features of Platforms and Markets
   ├── Demand for Single Network Good (Set 1)
   │   ├── Discrete 2 consumers (1.1)
   │   ├── Heterogeneous stand-alone (1.2)
   │   └── Heterogeneous network benefit (1.3)
   ├── Hotelling mit Network Effects (Set 2.1)
   ├── Compatibility-Decisions (Set 2.2)
   └── Defensibility, Data Network Effects

3. Growing a Platform
   ├── Chicken-and-Egg Problem
   ├── Divide-and-Conquer (Set 3.1)
   ├── Seeding Strategies (Marquee, Niche, Piggyback...)
   └── Building Trust

4. Platform Pricing & Disintermediation
   ├── Membership vs. Transaction Fees
   ├── Transaction Fees Numerical Example (Set 4.1)
   ├── Membership Fees General Model (Set 4.2–4.4)
   ├── Lerner-Index mit NE-Adjustment
   └── Disintermediation: Drivers & Counter-Strategies

5. Ratings, Reviews, and Recommendations
   ├── Product R&R vs. Seller R&R
   ├── Network Effects durch R&R
   ├── Trust-Game und Reputation
   ├── Empirie: Chevalier-Mayzlin, Luca, Resnick
   └── Challenges
```
